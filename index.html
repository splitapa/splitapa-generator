Exercises organized by functional criteria to support adapted and personalized workout plans.

From individual needs to practical programs.

A project developed to support movement professionals and practitioners with clear tools for adapted physical activity.

1

Analysis

Understand needs, goals, abilities, limitations, and priorities to choose suitable exercises.

2

3

Planning

Combine exercises to create clear, effective, and personalized plans.

4

Application
